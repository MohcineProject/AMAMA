Running as LocalSystem with this full token is expected and documented. The DLL evidence shows the binary loading from its legitimate path, and ntdll.dll from System32 — no anomalous DLL paths visible.

*No malfind hits, no network connections, no suspicious cmdline arguments.* The command line `/service` is the standard service invocation flag.
